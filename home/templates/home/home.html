What Services Are on Offer?

I provide a range of services that can be tailored to your needs, including:

Editorial Services

  • Manuscript Critiques
  • Developmental Editing
  • Copyediting
  • Proofreading

Editing and Proofreading Process

  1. Inquiry and Consultation:
    • A response to the inquiry, requesting the manuscript or document for review.
    • A free preliminary reading and consultation to assess needs and discuss the scope of the project.
    • A personalised quote is provided based on the type of editing required, word count, and any specific requests.
  2. Project Agreement and Payment:
    • Agreement to the terms and conditions and makes the payment (or arranges a payment plan).
    • A timeline is established, with clear deadlines for each stage of the process.
  3. Editing and Feedback:
    • The agreed-upon editing services (e.g., developmental editing, copyediting, proofreading) are completed.
    • The edited manuscript is returned to the client with clear and constructive feedback, using track changes and comments.
  4. Revisions and Finalisation:
    • The client reviews the edited manuscript and provides feedback or requests revisions.
    • Feedback is addressed and a revised version of the manuscript is provided.
    • Process may be repeated until the client is satisfied with the final product.
  5. Final Delivery:
    • The final manuscript is delivered in the requested format (e.g., Word document, PDF).
    • Dean Crystal provides ongoing support and answers any questions a client may have.

Formatting

  • Ebook Formatting
  • Print Formatting
  • Typesetting

Book Formatting Process

  1. Inquiry and Consultation:
    • Dean Crystal responds to the inquiry, requesting the manuscript and any relevant details (e.g., trim size, desired format).
    • A consultation is conducted to discuss the client's formatting preferences and goals.
    • A personalized quote is provided based on the complexity of the formatting and any additional services required (e.g., typesetting, cover design).
  2. Project Agreement and Payment:
    • The client agrees to the terms and conditions and makes the payment (or arranges a payment plan).
    • A timeline for the project is established, with clear deadlines for each stage of the formatting process.
  3. Formatting and Proofing:
    • Dean Crystal formats the manuscript according to industry standards and the client's preferences.
    • A proof copy is generated and reviewed for any errors or inconsistencies.
  4. Client Review and Revisions:
    • The client reviews the proof copy and provides feedback or requests revisions.
    • Dean Crystal addresses the client's feedback and provides a revised version of the formatted manuscript.
    • This process may be repeated until the client is satisfied with the final product.
  5. Final Delivery:
    • The final formatted files are delivered to the client in the requested formats (e.g., .mobi for Kindle, .epub for other ebook readers, PDF for print).
    • Dean Crystal provides guidance on publishing and distribution options.

Web Development

  • Website Design and Development
  • Content Management Systems

Full-Stack Web Development Process

  1. Inquiry and Consultation:
    • Dean Crystal responds to the inquiry, requesting details about the project (e.g., website type, features, design preferences).
    • A consultation is conducted to discuss the client's needs, goals, and budget.
    • A detailed proposal is provided outlining the project scope, timeline, and cost.
  2. Project Agreement and Payment:
    • The client agrees to the proposal and makes the payment (or arranges a payment plan).
    • A contract is signed outlining the project deliverables and timelines.
  3. Design and Development:
    • Dean Crystal creates wireframes and mockups to visualize the website's structure and design.
    • The client provides feedback and approves the design.
    • The website is developed using the agreed-upon technologies (e.g., Django, HTML, CSS, JavaScript).
    • Regular updates and progress reports are provided to the client.
  4. Testing and Deployment:
    • The website is thoroughly tested on various devices and browsers to ensure functionality and responsiveness.
    • The client reviews and approves the final version of the website.
    • The website is deployed to a hosting platform (e.g., Heroku, PythonAnywhere).
  5. Maintenance and Support:
    • Ongoing maintenance and support are provided as needed, including bug fixes, updates, and security patches.
    • Dean Crystal offers training and documentation to help the client manage their website
